An experimental character design project, featuring a variety of animations and scenes.

SHAKERS

Salt and pepper shakers concept. Opted for a monochrome palette to match the seasonings to the plastic used for 3D printing the prototypes. The form is inspired by a flower in a vase or a pestle in a mortar.

SCOOP LOW CHAIR

A scene featuring a replica of the Scoop Low Chair designed by Tom Dixon. Recreated the chair and experimented by creating a leather variation. It was placed in a desert scene with many arch shapes that compliment the curvature of the chair.

BUS STOP SHELTER

Bus stop shelter concept. A structure designed to provide shelter, withstand weather conditions, and adhere to city spacing requirements. The design is inspired by brutalist architecture and the form of birds. A polished blue and grey concrete material was used for most of the design. Spotlights are placed accordingly to provide lighting for when it is dark outside.
